Hi! I’m Chloe, a third year student at Northeastern University studying data science and biology. I built this site to share what I’m learning and creating. With AI there have been enormous progress in this area, especially in medicine, drug development and genomics. If something here sparks your interest, please reach out and learning from others. Thanks for stopping by!
